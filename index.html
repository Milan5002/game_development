Instructions

  • select the cups which contain coins after the positions of the cups are shifted.

Rules

  • 1. At the start of each level, the positions of the coins will be displayed for a few seconds.
  • 2. The golds will then be covered by paper cups, and the cups will start to move.
  • 3. You need to click the correct cups which contain coins in order to proceed.
  • 4. Be careful of tricky shifts, where the cups may move but the coins remain in the same spot.
  • 5. As you advance in the game, the number of coins will increase and the movements of the cups will become more complicated.
